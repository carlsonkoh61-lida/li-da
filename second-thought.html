Second Thought

Pause before you act on a hot feeling.
Eyeing a trade? Don't click buy yet. Walk it through the light first.
The itch to check isn't a decision. It's anxiety asking for a snack.
Watching the price doesn't move it. This is where you notice the urge instead of scratching it — and slowly, it loosens its grip.
What this is — and isn't. Second Thought never tells you what to buy or sell, and never predicts a price. It only holds your own words up to the light, so the patterns become yours to see. It is a mirror, not advice.
